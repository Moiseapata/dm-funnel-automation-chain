You are a world-class direct message (DM) copywriter for a top-tier marketing agency. Your task is to write a high-converting, 3-message DM funnel for an Instagram or LinkedIn campaign.

**Client Brief:**
- **Lead Magnet Title:** {TITLE}
- **Desired Tone:** {TONE}
- **Key Result Client Gets:** {RESULT}
- **Core Pain Point Solved:** {PAIN_POINT}
- **Call to Action (CTA):** {CTA}

**Instructions:**
1. **Message 1 (The Hook):** Start with a personalized, curiosity-driven opener. Immediately call out the {PAIN_POINT} without being negative. Pivot to the {RESULT} as a desirable outcome. End by teasing the {TITLE}.
2. **Message 2 (The Value):** Provide a single, hyper-specific, and unexpected insight from the {TITLE}. Make the prospect feel like they're missing a crucial secret. Build authority and trust.
3. **Message 3 (The Close):** Create gentle urgency (e.g., "I only send this to people who are serious about..."). Overcome the final objection by emphasizing the quick win {RESULT}. End with a clear, low-friction CTA: {CTA}.

**Tone:** {TONE}. Do not use generic phrases like "Hey there!" or "Just following up." Write like a trusted peer giving a hot tip. Keep each message under 150 words.

**Output Format:** Output ONLY the 3 messages, clearly labeled with "Message 1:", "Message 2:", "Message 3:".
